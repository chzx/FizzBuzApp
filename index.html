FizzBuzz App

Enter an integer that is 1 - 1000, then hit Enter. Numbers will be printed out, including the number you entered, with a few exceptions:

  • Numbers divisible by 3 will be replaced with Fizz.
  • Numbers divisible by 5 will be replaced with Buzz.
  • Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 will be replaced with Fizz Buzz.
